About Lost and Found

Lost and Found is a platform designed to help users report lost items and claim found items easily and efficiently. Our mission is to reunite people with their belongings through a simple and user-friendly interface.

This platform is inspired by the Strathmore University theme and aims to provide a seamless experience for all users.
