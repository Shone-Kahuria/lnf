About Lost and Found

Lost and Found is a platform designed to help users report lost items and claim found items easily and efficiently. Our mission is to reunite people with their belongings through a simple and user-friendly interface.

This platform is inspired by the Strathmore University theme and aims to provide a seamless experience for all users.

Our Values

  • Trust: We ensure that all reported items are handled with care and confidentiality.
  • Accessibility: Our platform is easy to use for everyone, including those with disabilities.
  • Community: We foster a supportive environment to help people recover their lost belongings.

How It Works

  1. Report your lost item with detailed information.
  2. Browse found items reported by others.
  3. Contact support for assistance or to claim items.
