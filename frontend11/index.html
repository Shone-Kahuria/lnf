Welcome to Lost and Found

Report lost items or claim found items easily.

How It Works

Use the navigation above to report lost items, view found items, or contact us for assistance.

  1. Report Lost Item: Navigate to the "Report Item" page and fill out the form with details about your lost item.
  2. View Found Items: Check the "Found Items" page regularly to see if your lost item has been found and listed.
  3. Contact Us: If you need help or want to claim an item, use the "Contact" page to reach out to our support team.

Tips for Using Lost and Found

  • Provide as much detail as possible when reporting lost items to increase chances of recovery.
  • Check the found items list regularly for updates.
  • Contact support promptly if you find a match or need help.
